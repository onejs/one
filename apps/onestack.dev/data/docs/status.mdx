This is a live document covering the high level status of the various parts of One. We don't cover all areas, just high level stable, and all developing or unstable sub-areas.

Broadly: web is stable, and native is stable in Metro mode.

### Native <Status is="stable" />

Native is fully stable in [Metro mode](/docs/metro-mode). Every feature is stable and ready for production, including building to production and deploying to app stores, as it is in every way a normal Metro-built app.

In Vite-native mode, we are waiting for some Rolldown APIs to continue our work. Currently, it is working but paused:

- Local development <Status is="stable" />
- Production builds <Status is="stable" />
- Assets <Status is="developing" />
- Symbolicator <Status is="early" />
